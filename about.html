About Cylingas

One of the UAE's longest-established EPC & fabrication specialists.

More than five decades engineering, fabricating and constructing industrial assets for the Oil & Gas, energy and infrastructure sectors — from a single Dubai workshop to a regional contractor.

Company Profile

Engineering capability, proven at industrial scale.

Cylingas is an EPC and fabrication company operating for more than 50 years, with an approximately 300,000 sq ft facility in Dubai, a skilled workforce and complete infrastructure — including the in-house capability to roll plate up to 70 mm thick.

We deliver the design, engineering and fabrication of Oil & Gas storage tanks, spheres, API 650 and 620 vertical storage tanks, pressure vessels, process equipment, pipeline spools and steel products.

Association with renowned international brands, strict adherence to HSE requirements, and a wide customer base stand as testimony to our commitment to quality across five decades.

Our History

From 1974 to today.

2024

Awarded EPC for Aviation Fuel Storage by ADNOC, and supply of spheres for the NMDC / Técnicas Reunidas ADNOC MERAM project.

2022

Awarded EPC project for cryogenic ethylene storage tanks.

2021

Awarded EPC storage tanks at the ADNOC BFIP project (via CPECC).

2020

Awarded storage tank projects at ADNOC Dabbayia.

2019

Prequalified with ADNOC as an EPC / Fabrication contractor.

2018

Acquired by private investors with a focus on medium-to-large EPC contracts.

2013

Opened the wider GCC export market.

2010

EPC division established with a focus on storage facility projects.

1980

On-site construction of API 650 tanks and AWWA D100 water tanks.

1976

Production startup — manufacture of gas cylinders and pressure vessels.

1974

Company inception by decree of H.H. Sheikh Rashid Bin Saeed Al Maktoum.
